Timetable

Our timetable will be available 6 weeks before the opening ceremony on August, 26th, 2022!

What we have planned so far:

  • Each guest will participate in the opening and closing ceremonies
  • Each guest will participate in at least one panel and all guests will have a panel of their own
  • Guests will be available for autographs throughout the event when they are not at a panel and outside of photoshoot sessions
  • There will be designated times for photoshoot sessions for each guest on each day of the convention
  • There will be several panels including multiple guests
    • We will have one Star Trek panel including John de Lancie, Jason Isaacs, Anthony Rapp, and Wilson Cruz
    • Seth MacFarlane and Scott Grimes will hold an Orville panel
    • John de Lancie and John Barrowman will hold a mischiefmakers-in-chief panel
    • Krysten Ritter, Elizabeth Olsen, Summer Glau, and Dominique Tipper will hold a women in SciFi panel
    • Anthony Rapp and Wilson Cruz will hold a gayness in SciFi panel
    • Stay tuned for more panels!
